ABOUT US

The Live Music Theatre Company is a nonprofit center for the arts and culture located in the heart of downtown Maryville, Missouris. We bring together a community of musicians, actors, artists, writers, fans, and supporters who are dedicated to the simple premise that the broadest spectrum of live entertainment and cultural exchange is essential to the vitality of our society.
